# YeVault

A comprehensive archive of every Ye era: unreleased, released, and album copies. Browse songs by era, view quality ratings, and access direct download/stream links.

---

## Version History

### v2.2.1
- Reverted to no-artwork display for stability

### v2.2.0
- Added album artwork support per era
- Integrated MusicBrainz Cover Art Archive for official releases
- Direct image links for unreleased era artwork

### v2.0.0

- Era rows with song counts and expandable song lists
- Quality badges (High Quality, CD Quality, Low Quality, etc.)
- Hero section with per-era color theming
- Song count and era count stats
